Tenzir v4.16

June 5, 2024 · 3 min read
Dominik Lohmann
Engineering Manager

Pipelines now connect more flexibly than ever before with Tenzir v4.16 and its upgraded publish and subscribe operators.

Multi-Producer Multi-Consumer​

This approach, however, fell apart as soon as another data source tried to publish to the topic alerts. Trying to do so just displayed an error. Fundamentally, the publish and subscribe operators were single-producer, multi-consumer (SPMC).
