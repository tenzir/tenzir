Tenzir Node v4.22

October 18, 2024 · 2 min read
Jannis Christopher Köhl
Software Engineer
Raghav Narang
Software Engineer

Tenzir Node v4.22 comes with documentation for the new version of the Tenzir Query Language, connectors for Google Cloud Pub/Sub and various bug fixes.

TQL2 Documentation​

We are thrilled to share that the new version of our query language, TQL2, has taken exciting strides forward! If you’ve been using the Tenzir Platform, you might have already noticed the toggle to enable TQL2, allowing you to explore its potential.

Today marks a significant milestone as we release the initial version of our TQL2 documentation. You can now dive deep into learning TQL2 and unlock its full capabilities!

We’re committed to continuously enhancing the documentation, ensuring you have all the resources you need to make the most of TQL2. Stay tuned for more updates!

Google Cloud Pub/Sub Integration​

Introducing our latest connector: Google Cloud Pub/Sub! The new google-cloud-pubsub connector allows users to seamlessly subscribe and publish to Google Cloud Pub/Sub.

The connector is also available in TQL2 as load_google_cloud_pubsub and save_google_cloud_pubsub:

Other Changes​

This release additionally includes numerous small bug fixes and under-the-hood improvements. For a detailed list of changes, be sure to check out the changelog.

Join Us for Office Hours​

Every second Tuesday at 5 PM CET, we hold our office hours on our Discord server. Whether you have ideas for new packages or want to discuss upcoming features—join us for a chat!
